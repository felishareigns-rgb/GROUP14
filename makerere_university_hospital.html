About Makerere University Hospital

Serving Uganda with excellence since 1972

Our Mission

Makerere University Hospital is a leading academic medical centre affiliated with Makerere University in Kampala, Uganda. We provide high-quality, accessible healthcare to all patients while training the next generation of medical professionals.

Our hospital combines cutting-edge research with compassionate patient care. We serve thousands of patients annually across more than 30 clinical departments, offering everything from routine outpatient consultations to complex surgical procedures.

Our History

Founded in 1972, the hospital has grown from a small teaching facility into one of Uganda's premier medical institutions. Over five decades, we have continuously expanded our services and improved our facilities to meet the growing healthcare needs of our community.
